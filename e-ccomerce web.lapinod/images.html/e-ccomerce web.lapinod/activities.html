    • -Offer personalized consultations for bridesmaids to help them choose the perfect dresses that complement the bride's gown and fit their individual styles and preferences. Provide expert advice on color coordination, styles, and accessories.

    • Organize a gown donation drive where customers can donate their gently used gowns for a charitable cause. Partner with a local organization or charity to distribute the gowns to those in need, such as underprivileged brides or individuals attending special events.
