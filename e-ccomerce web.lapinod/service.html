Our Services

1.Gown Design: Providing personalized gown design services where customers can work closely with a designer to create a unique gown that fits their specific preferences, body measurements, and event requirements. This service may involve consultations, fabric selection, sketching, and multiple fittings to ensure the gown meets the customer's expectations.

2. Alterations and Tailoring: Offering alteration and tailoring services to adjust the fit, length, or style of gowns to suit individual customers. This could involve resizing, hemming, adding or removing embellishments, or modifying the neckline or sleeves to achieve the desired look and fit.

3.Gown Rental: Providing a gown rental service where customers can rent designer gowns for special occasions such as weddings, galas, or proms. This service may include a wide selection of gowns in various sizes and styles, fittings, and rental agreements.

4.Gown Cleaning and Preservation: Offering professional cleaning and preservation services for gowns, ensuring they are properly cleaned, steamed, and stored to maintain their quality and longevity. This service may be particularly valuable for customers who want to preserve their cherished gowns as keepsakes or for future use.

5.Styling and Consultation: Providing expert styling advice and consultation to customers, helping them choose the perfect gown that complements their body shape, skin tone, and event theme. This service may involve personalized recommendations, trying on different gown options, and providing guidance on accessories and overall styling.

6.Gown Showcase or Fashion Events: Organizing gown showcases or fashion events where customers can view and try on the latest gown collections. This service allows customers to explore different gown designs, interact with designers, and make informed choices based on their preferences
